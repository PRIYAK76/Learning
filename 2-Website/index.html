PARK CITY'S FINEST

The Bakery aspires to be more than simply the finest bakery in Park City. We hold ourselves to elevated standards of quality in our foods to ensure that each item we offer is a delight to both the eye and the palate. We strive for consistency so that each visit to our bakery delivers the same reliably satisfying experience while offering an exciting variety of products. Our products change to showcase seasonal ingredients and the exceptional creative talents of our chefs.

SWEET + SAVORY

Our team works hard every day to provide a
range of bites to satisfy every palate.
Our team creates artisan breads, French-inspired pastries, and a full line of savory gourmet take-out, including soups, salads and entrees. We also offer house made specialty cakes, pies and tarts. In addition to retail offerings, Windy Ridge Bakery also provides all of the breads, desserts and ice creams that everyone can enjoy at all of our restaurants in the Bill White Restaurants group here in Park City.
As we expand our offerings of prepared foods to complement our baked goods, The Bakery aims to be considered the first choice of discerning customers seeking quality take-home meals. A commitment to service that is friendly, helpful and passionate about food is the companion to our devotion to quality by which we endeavor to guarantee each customer the highest possible satisfaction.
